Minidome
The Minidome's webviewer is a web-based interface. It allows users to interact with the digitized objects through the web withouth the need for installing specific software or the need for specific operating systems.

Browser requirements

This viewer is based on the state-of-the-art WebGL API for rendering 3D graphics through your browser. Altough quite some progress is made to have WebGL standard on all browsers, whether or not it will work for you will depend on what browser and which version you use. The webviewer has been tested for Google Chrome, Apple Safari and Mozilla Firefox. Until further notice, Internet Explorer does not support WebGl unfortunately. Make sure 'WebGL' is activated in your brower. You can verify whether your browser and graphics supports WebGL through http://get.webgl.org/. If you still have trouble, contact info@minidome.be.

Controls

The interactive visualization is controlled by mouse/keyboard or touch input. Specific visualization modes, as indicated in the Display tab on the right hand side of the screen, are set by clicking the corresponding buttons.
